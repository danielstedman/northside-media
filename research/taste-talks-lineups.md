# Taste Talks Lineups: A Year-by-Year History (2013-2017)

## 2013 (September 13-15, Brooklyn)

**Curator:** April Bloomfield (The Spotted Pig)

**Notable Participants:** Dan Barber, Mario Batali, Wylie Dufresne, Questlove, Andrew Tarlow, The Mast Brothers

**Events:** All-Star Cookout at East River State Park, Cook It Raw Brooklyn partnership with international chefs

---

## 2014

### Brooklyn (September 12-14)

**Curator:** Danny Bowien (Mission Chinese)

**Notable Participants:** Mario Batali, Christina Tosi, Dale Talde, Andy Ricker, Action Bronson, Alex Guarnaschelli, Amanda Hesser, Sam Sifton, Adam Rapoport (Bon Appetit EIC), Christine Muhlke, Kate Krader, Ken Friedman, Brooks Headley

Action Bronson debuted "Fuck, That's Delicious" at Taste Talks. The Future Food Expo launched as a free event.

**Venues:** Colossal Media, East River Park

### Chicago (October 3-5)

First expansion outside Brooklyn.

**Curator:** Paul Kahan (Blackbird, Avec)

**Notable Participants:** Stephanie Izard, Matty Matheson, Jeni Britton Bauer

---

## 2015 (September 11-13, Brooklyn)

**Curator:** Questlove

George Takei appeared at the BBQ event. The Kellogg's Cereal Throwdown was won by Sam Mason.

**Notable Participants:** Dominique Ansel, Matty Matheson, Marco Canora, Leah Cohen, Alex Stupak, Lea DeLaria, Andy Ricker, DJ Premier

**Venues:** Wythe Hotel, Kinfolk Studios

---

## 2016

### Brooklyn (September 9-11)

**Curator:** Sean Brock (Husk)

**Notable Participants:** Wylie Dufresne, Gabrielle Hamilton, Christina Tosi, Danny Bowien, Brooks Headley

Featured panel: "The Shape of Food to Come"

### Chicago (September 30 - October 2)

**Curator:** Jason Hammel (Lula Cafe)

Glenn Kotche (Wilco) collaborated on a multi-sensory dinner.

**Notable Participants:** Andy Ricker, Paul Liebrandt

### Los Angeles (November 4-6, Inaugural)

**Curator:** Kelis

**Notable Participants:** Curtis Stone, Kris Yenbamroong, Brooke Williamson, Suzanne Tracht

**Venue:** The Line Hotel

### The Tastys (Inaugural)

The Tastys awards ceremony launched at the BAM Howard Gilman Opera House, hosted by Mo Rocca. 28 categories were awarded.

---

## 2017

### Brooklyn (September 8-10)

**Curator:** Wylie Dufresne

**Notable Participants:** Dan Barber, Floyd Cardoz, Andrew Carmellini, Greg Baxtrom (Olmsted)

The festival expanded to four cities: Brooklyn, Chicago, LA, and Miami.

### The Tastys (2nd Ceremony)

Held at The Weylin, hosted by Matty Matheson.

**Winners:**
- Outstanding Restaurant: Blue Hill at Stone Barns
- Best Cookbook: Samin Nosrat, "Salt Fat Acid Heat"
- Best Startup: Impossible Foods
- Best Magazine: Lucky Peach
- Best Online Media: Eater
