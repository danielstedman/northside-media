# Northside Festival Lineups: A Year-by-Year History (2009-2018)

## 2009 (June 11-14) — Inaugural Year

Approximately 100+ acts across a handful of Williamsburg/Greenpoint venues in the festival's inaugural year. Registration took place in the backroom of an art gallery that had been booked just 12 hours before. Presented by The L Magazine. Badges were $45.

**Venues:** Music Hall of Williamsburg, Studio B, Public Assembly, GlassLands, Spike Hill, Cameo Gallery, and others

**Showcase Curators:** BrooklynVegan, Frenchkiss Records, Gothamist, WFUV/The Alternate Side, Kanine Records, Sleep When Dead, KEXP, Sound Fix Records, Ernest Jenning Record Co., Earfarm, Ninja Tune Records, NYCTaper, and others

## 2014 (June 12-15)

400+ bands across 50+ venues, the festival's largest footprint. The "100 Most Influential" list debuted. CHVRCHES played a free McCarren Park show. Shamir made his live debut. War on Drugs arrived fresh off the release of Lost in the Dream. Death by Audio and Glasslands were in their final months of operation.

**Notable Artists:** War on Drugs, Beirut, CHVRCHES (free McCarren Park), Sharon Van Etten, Courtney Barnett, Titus Andronicus, Talib Kweli, Perfect Pussy, Pharmakon, Alvvays, Shamir (live debut), BADBADNOTGOOD

---

## 2015 (June 11-14)

350+ artists. Run the Jewels headlined 50 Kent with Sleigh Bells and Vince Staples. Against Me! played a free McCarren Park show. Pusha T performed at House of Vans. The festival was acquired by Zealot Networks.

**Notable Artists:** Run the Jewels + Sleigh Bells + Vince Staples (50 Kent), Neko Case, Best Coast, Built to Spill, Against Me! (free McCarren Park), Blonde Redhead, Pusha T (House of Vans), DJ Premier, Mitski, Princess Nokia, Sun Ra Arkestra, Ex Hex, Alvvays, Bully, Rhye

---

## 2016 (June 9-12 music / June 6-12 full festival)

400+ performers. Brian Wilson performed Pet Sounds in its entirety at McCarren Park for the album's 50th anniversary. Wolf Parade reunited for a free McCarren Park show. Kacey Musgraves, Conor Oberst, and the Felice Brothers shared a McCarren Park bill.

**Notable Artists:** Brian Wilson performing Pet Sounds in entirety (McCarren Park, 50th anniversary), Wolf Parade (reunion, free McCarren Park), Kacey Musgraves + Conor Oberst + Felice Brothers (McCarren Park), Grandmaster Flash, Sunflower Bean, Hinds, King Khan & the Shrines, Diet Cig, Subhumans

---

## 2017 (June 8-11)

300+ bands across 34 venues. Badges were $75. The company repurchased the festival from Zealot Networks. Lower Dens performed ABBA's Gold in full. Mary Timony played Helium material. Thursday reunited.

**Notable Artists:** Miguel (McCarren Park headliner), Dirty Projectors, Kamasi Washington, Thursday (reunion), Big Thief, Julia Holter, Jlin, William Basinski, PUP, Jeff Rosenstock, Vagabon, Lower Dens performing ABBA's Gold in full, Mary Timony plays Helium

---

## 2018 (June 7-10) — Final Festival

300+ artists across 30+ venues. The final Northside Festival. For the first time, there were no McCarren Park outdoor shows. Liz Phair performed Exile in Guyville in its entirety at National Sawdust for the album's 25th anniversary, with Soccer Mommy opening. Parquet Courts played a Rocks Off boat cruise. The festival would not return.

**Notable Artists:** Liz Phair performing Exile in Guyville in entirety (25th anniversary, National Sawdust) with Soccer Mommy opening, Snail Mail (sold out MHOW), Protomartyr, Deerhoof, La Luz, Parquet Courts (Rocks Off boat cruise), Jeff Parker, Makaya McCraven, Irreversible Entanglements, Standing on the Corner, Lou Barlow, Pissed Jeans
