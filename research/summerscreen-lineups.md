# SummerScreen Lineups: A Year-by-Year History (2006-2018)

## 2006 (Inaugural)

The first SummerScreen season at McCarren Park Pool.

**Films:** Do the Right Thing, Bottle Rocket, The French Connection, The Swimmer, Dog Day Afternoon, Style Wars

---

## 2007

Eight films screened in the second season.

**Films:** Bring It On, Night of the Hunter, Dazed and Confused, Ladies and Gentlemen the Fabulous Stains, Repo Man, Three Kings, Bonnie and Clyde, Purple Rain

---

## 2008

A $3 suggested donation was introduced.

**Films:** Rushmore, The Virgin Suicides, Wet Hot American Summer, Desperately Seeking Susan, Mean Streets, 28 Days Later, Velvet Goldmine, Blue Velvet

---

## 2009

**Films:** Reality Bites, Evil Dead 2, 24 Hour Party People, Wild at Heart, Fame, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

---

## 2010

**Films:** Say Anything, Labyrinth, Romeo + Juliet, Dead Man, Starship Troopers, Point Break

**Bands:** Grooms, Food Stamps

---

## 2011

Presented by L Magazine and EPIX.

**Films:** Friday, Wayne's World, Ghost World, Ferris Bueller's Day Off, Clueless, Jurassic Park

---

## 2012

**Films:** Cruel Intentions, Raising Arizona, Dirty Dancing, The Princess Bride, Top Gun, Audience Choice: Empire Records

---

## 2013

**Films:** Can't Hardly Wait, Pee Wee's Big Adventure, The Craft, The Goonies, Speed, Audience Pick: The NeverEnding Story

**Bands:** Jeffrey Lewis, Oberhofer, Ratking, Amen Dunes

---

## 2014

Music curated by Todd P.

**Films:** Back to the Future, Zoolander, Cry-Baby, Heathers, The Big Lebowski, Audience Choice: Spice World

**Music (curated by Todd P):** Tonstartssbandht, The Dreebs, Ratking, Princess Nokia, Man Forever, GOASTT (Sean Lennon), Kleenex Girl Wonder, DJ Dog Dick, Eartheater

---

## 2015 (10th Anniversary "Greatest Hits")

The season celebrated 10 years with a "Greatest Hits" lineup of returning fan favorites. A Todd P controversy erupted when he pulled all bands after a Vitamin Water branding deal was announced. Worriers replaced the originally booked opening night act.

**Films:** Clueless, Wet Hot American Summer, Dirty Dancing, Dazed and Confused, Jurassic Park, Audience Pick

---

## 2016

**Films:** Scream, Harold and Kumar Go to White Castle, The Fast and the Furious, 10 Things I Hate About You, The Royal Tenenbaums, Audience Pick

**Bands:** Juliana Huxtable, LVL UP, Cotillion

---

## 2017 ("So Fetch")

Music curated by Tidal.

**Films:** Mean Girls, Office Space, Donnie Darko, Selena, I Know What You Did Last Summer, Audience Choice

**Bands:** Yucky Duster, Gobbinjr

---

## 2018 (Final Season)

The longest season ever with eight screenings. SummerScreen would not return.

**Films:** Jawbreaker, When Harry Met Sally, Love & Basketball, Hackers, Fantastic Mr. Fox, Die Hard, Musical Movie TBA, Audience Choice
