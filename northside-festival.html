Northside Festival

Brooklyn's Discovery Festival · 2009–2018

What began with 50 bands in a back room booked 12 hours before opening grew into 400+ artists across 50+ venues in Williamsburg, Greenpoint, and Bushwick. Over ten years, Northside Festival hosted first-ever shows, reunion sets, and career-defining performances — from Chance the Rapper's first NYC show to Brian Wilson performing Pet Sounds in its entirety. Reuters called it "the SXSW of the East Coast."

June 11–14 · ~50 acts · Inaugural year
