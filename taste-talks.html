Taste Talks

Food and Culture. Not Just Foodie Culture.

Brooklyn · Chicago · Los Angeles · Miami · 2013–2017

Taste Talks launched in Brooklyn and grew into a national food festival spanning four cities. Curated by some of the most influential figures in food — April Bloomfield, Danny Bowien, Questlove, Sean Brock, Kelis, Wylie Dufresne — and featuring everyone from Mario Batali to Action Bronson.

2013

Brooklyn · September 13–15

Curator: April Bloomfield (The Spotted Pig)

All-Star Cookout at East River State Park with 25 celebrity chefs. Cook It Raw Brooklyn brought international chefs for an impromptu outdoor meal using locally sourced ingredients.

Dan Barber, Mario Batali, Wylie Dufresne, Questlove, Andrew Tarlow, The Mast Brothers, Nate Smith, Sean Rembold, Eli Sussman, Dave 1 of Chromeo, Sam Anderson, Frank Castronovo

2014

Brooklyn (Sept 12–14) + Chicago (Oct 3–5)

Brooklyn: Danny Bowien (Mission Chinese)
Chicago: Paul Kahan (Blackbird, Avec)

Action Bronson debuted "Fuck, That's Delicious" — which became a major Viceland series. The free Future Food Expo launched. First expansion outside Brooklyn.

Mario Batali, Christina Tosi, Action Bronson, Dale Talde, Andy Ricker, Brooks Headley, Alex Guarnaschelli, Michael White, Amanda Hesser, Sara Moulton, Adam Rapoport, Sam Sifton, Peter Meehan, Christine Muhlke, Kate Krader, Ken Friedman, Lee Tiernan, Phil Ward

Chicago: Stephanie Izard, Matty Matheson, Doug Psaltis, Jeni Britton Bauer

2015

Brooklyn · September 11–13

Curator: Questlove (The Roots / Tonight Show)

George Takei appeared at the All-Star BBQ. Questlove DJ'd 80s-inspired sets. Kellogg's Cereal Throwdown won by Sam Mason. Rooftop Dessert Party with Dominique Ansel.

Dominique Ansel, George Takei, Matty Matheson, Marco Canora, Leah Cohen, Daniel Holtzman, Alex Stupak, Lea DeLaria, Andy Ricker, Ivy Mix, Russell Jackson, Sam Mason, Hugue Dufour, Frank Falcinelli, Frank Castronovo

2016

Brooklyn + Chicago + Los Angeles (inaugural)

Brooklyn: Sean Brock (Husk)
Chicago: Jason Hammel (Lula Cafe)
LA: Kelis

Glenn Kotche (Wilco) collaborated with Hammel on a multi-sensory dinner pairing food with music and visuals. The Tastys awards launched at BAM Howard Gilman Opera House, hosted by Mo Rocca.

Brooklyn: Wylie Dufresne, Gabrielle Hamilton, Christina Tosi, Danny Bowien, Brooks Headley, Jeremiah Stone, Fabian von Hauske, Matthew Hyland, Billy Durney

Chicago: Glenn Kotche, Andy Ricker, Paul Liebrandt, Chris Pandel, Charlie McKenna, Diana Davila

LA: Curtis Stone, Kris Yenbamroong, Brooke Williamson, Suzanne Tracht, Chris Oh, Nyesha Arrington

2017

Four cities: Brooklyn, Chicago, LA, Miami

Curator: Wylie Dufresne (wd~50, Du's Donuts)

Dan Barber spoke about "hedonism and the future of food." The Tastys 2nd ceremony at The Weylin, hosted by Matty Matheson. Expanded to four cities.

Dan Barber, Matty Matheson, Floyd Cardoz, Andrew Carmellini, Greg Baxtrom, Ariane Daguin, Dana Cowin, Jamie Young, Timon Balloo, Jeremiah Stone, Fabian von Hauske

The Tastys 2017

Awards ceremony at The Weylin, Brooklyn. Hosted by Matty Matheson.
